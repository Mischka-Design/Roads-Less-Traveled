4 Day Private Safari Experience

This private safari tour takes you to one of South Africa’s best conservation areas, The Greater Makalali Private Game Reserve...

Description

This private safari tour takes you to one of South Africa’s best conservation areas, The Greater Makalali Private Game Reserve, spanning over 20,000 hectares.

Makalali is part of the Great Kruger Area. It is home to 135 mammal species, all of Africa's Big 5 and is renowned for unique sightings of the elusive Brown Hyena and Black Rhino.

My Khaya, Your Khaya is an exclusive use lodge that gives friends and families the opportunity to enjoy their own unique Safari experience all to themselves.

Itinerary

Arrival

  • You will be collected from O.R. Tambo International Airport.

  • Accommodation before the tour starts can be arranged at an extra cost.

Day 1

On arrival in Johannesburg, you will be met after baggage collection by your host/guide who will be with you for your travel adventures.

You will be transferred in a private vehicle to Makalali Private Game Reserve, please note that this journey will take around 5 hours. You will be stopping along the way to stretch your legs and you will have the opportunity to buy lunch (at your own expense).

Arrival will be mid afternoon at My Khaya Your Khaya, your exclusive use camp for the duration of your stay on Makalali Private Game Reserve. While you settle in your guide will prepare a refreshing drink and if everyone is up for it you can jump onto your first game drive in an open game viewer to explore the beauty of the bush!

On return to the camp your guide and host will prepare a traditional South African braai for you to enjoy in the outdoor dining area, known locally as a Boma.

Day 2

An early morning start as we head out to explore Makalali at sunrise. We will have coffee, tea and rusks on board and even blankets and hot water bottles for cold winter mornings. It is often in the early hours that we find the most animal activity. After what we hope will be some amazing sightings, we will head back to your camp where a sumptuous breakfast will be served. Enjoy some time to relax and take in the view and surroundings on the banks of the Makhutswi River, listen to the sounds of the bush and if you are lucky view some game in the dry riverbed below your camp. Sightings of elephants, lions and plains game are not uncommon at My Khaya Your Khaya. After lunch we will continue our search for wildlife and return to camp at sunset. Tonight, we will be cooking another true South African favorite, a Potjie.

Day 3

This morning you will be going on a bush walk (not for under 16's) where you’ll learn basic tracking techniques, how to identify various tracks and signs as well as gain a better understanding of the fauna and flora of the region. You can also choose to go on a solitude walk, where you’ll walk in complete silence as your guide silently points out things along the way. At the end of the walk, you’ll talk about the things you saw and have a moment of quiet reflection to absorb your beautiful natural surroundings. Back in camp there will be a lovely breakfast served , after which you have some time at your leisure. Post lunch we will head out for a game drive to see what else you can still find on your African adventure! We will return to camp just after sunset. Dinner tonight will be another meal made on the open fire.

Day 4

Sadly, your private Safari has come to an end. After breakfast, you will head back to Johannesburg. We will take you back via the magnificent Panorama Route along the Blyde River Canyon, where you will have the opportunity to take in some of South Africa’s most scenic views from the Northern Drakensberg Mountains. We'll head through Pilgrim's Rest for a quick lunch break (at your own expense), before hitting the road for the last leg to Jo'burg. You will arrive at O.R. Tambo International Airport at approximately 17H00.

End of Tour
  • You'll be dropped off at O.R. Tambo International Airport
  • Additional accommodation can be arranged at an extra cost if you require an overnight before your flight out.
