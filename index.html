Roads Less Traveled

Since 2016

Roads Less Traveled Africa specializes in luxury tours that take you a little further off the beaten track than most. We offer fully bespoke, private touring with a luxurious element to make for a really special day. Whether you are a honeymoon couple or a multi-generational family we will tailor a tour to suit your needs. All our guides are qualified cultural guides with a background in safari guiding. This gives them a broader knowledge and understanding of the area in which we operate!
