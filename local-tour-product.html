Local Community & Village Interactions

Take either a full or part day out to visit one of our community project partners and discover what it is being done on the ground to empower the local communities when it comes to their health, education and overall well being.

Description

Take either a full or part day out to visit one of our community project partners and discover what it is being done on the ground to empower the local communities when it comes to their health, education and overall well being.

Enjoy interacting with the kids from the local school's and take a village tour to meet a sangoma and members of the community. It's a fun excursion and your visit will be contributing in a positive way both financially and culturally.
